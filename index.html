About Us

At Vayu Clean Technologies, we are committed to creating cutting-edge solutions that ensure the highest standards of cleanliness and contamination control across a wide range of industries. As a trusted manufacturer of high-performance clean room equipment, we specialize in designing and delivering precision-engineered products that meet the strictest regulatory and environmental requirements.

With a focus on innovation, quality, and reliability, our product range includes laminar airflow units, biosafety cabinets, pass boxes, air showers, and other critical cleanroom solutions. Every product we deliver is a result of rigorous engineering, advanced manufacturing practices, and a deep understanding of our clients’ needs in sectors such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, healthcare, electronics, and research.

Backed by a team of experienced professionals and a customer-centric approach, Vayu Clean Technologies has built a reputation for excellence, integrity, and long-term value. We don’t just manufacture equipment—we build environments where safety, precision, and performance thrive.

Join us in our mission to make the world cleaner, safer, and more efficient—one cleanroom at a time.
